Sec. 13.462. LICENSE RENEWAL. A person licensed under this
subchapter must periodically renew the person's license. The license
expires unless the license holder submits an application for renewal
accompanied by the renewal fee set by the department or by the late
fee set by the department and meets the requirements for renewal.
