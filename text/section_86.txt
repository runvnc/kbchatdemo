Sec. 12A.151. EXAMINATION PROCEDURES. For each licensing
examination administered by the department, the department shall:
(1) adopt policies and guidelines detailing the procedures
for the testing process, including test admission and internal test
administration procedures; and
(2) post on the department's Internet website the policies
that reference the testing procedures.
Added by Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 506 (S.B. 1016), Sec. 6.03,
eff. September 1, 2009.
