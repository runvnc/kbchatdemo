Sec. 51.015. NOTES AS SUBSCRIPTION CONTRACTS. Notes given for
membership certificates of a society are valid subscription contracts
and are the property of the society.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1099, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
