Sec. 52.084. NOTICE OF MEETINGS. Not later than the 10th day
before the day of a meeting of a marketing association, the
association shall:
(1) mail to each member notice of the meeting and a
statement of the purpose of the meeting; or
(2) if the bylaws so provide, publish notice of the meeting
in a newspaper of general circulation in the area in which the
association's principal place of business is located.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1110, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
