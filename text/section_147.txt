Sec. 13.460. INSURANCE POLICY REQUIRED FOR SERVICE COMPANY. A
service company shall maintain at all times while the service company
performs device maintenance activities a current effective operations
liability insurance policy issued by an insurance company authorized
to do business in this state or by a surplus lines insurer that meets
the requirements of Chapter 981, Insurance Code, and rules adopted by
the commissioner of insurance in an amount set by the department and
based on the type of licensed activities to be performed.
Added by Acts 2013, 83rd Leg., R.S., Ch. 924 (H.B. 1494), Sec. 3.26,
eff. September 1, 2013.
