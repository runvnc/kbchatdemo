Sec. 71.102. FIELD INSPECTION. The department shall conduct
field inspections of vegetable plants and certify those plants prior
to the preparation for shipment in order to provide the purchaser of
the plants with an honest and reliable opinion on the freedom of the
plants from disease and fungus infection and insect infestation and
to ensure the proper packaging and handling of certified plants.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1160, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
