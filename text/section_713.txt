Sec. 62.0021. MEETINGS BY TELEPHONE CONFERENCE CALL. (a)
Notwithstanding Chapter 551, Government Code, the board may hold an
open or closed meeting by telephone conference call if immediate
action is required and the convening at one location of a quorum of
the board is inconvenient for any member of the board.
(b) The meeting is subject to the notice requirements
applicable to other meetings.
(c) The notice of the meeting must specify as the location of
the meeting the location where meetings of the board are usually
held.
(d) Each part of the meeting that is required to be open to the
public shall be audible to the public at the location specified in
the notice of the meeting as the location of the meeting and shall be
tape-recorded. The tape recording shall be made available to the
public.
Added by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 74, Sec. 1, eff. May 5, 1993.
Amended by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 76, Sec. 5.95(78), eff. Sept. 1,
1995.
