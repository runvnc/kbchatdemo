Sec. 201.202. USE OF FUNDS. (a) The State Soil and Water
Conservation Board shall use money from the agricultural soil and
water conservation fund for providing technical assistance to
landowners and operators for soil and water conservation land
improvement measures and soil and water conservation plans developed
jointly by landowners and operators and local soil and water
conservation districts.
(b) The board shall designate priorities among the various land
improvement measures, including:
(1) brush control and other water supply enhancement
activities;
(2) forest improvement measures;
(3) returning erosive cropland to pasture and other
practices that maximize water conservation;
