Sec. 141.126. PROSECUTIONS. Each district attorney, criminal
district attorney, or county attorney to whom the service reports a
violation of this chapter shall cause appropriate proceedings to be
instituted and prosecuted in the proper court without delay in the
manner provided by law.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1332, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Renumbered from Sec. 141.125 and amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p.
1876, ch. 349, art. 2, Sec. 8, eff. Sept. 1, 1983.
