Sec. 95.033. NONCOMPLYING CITRUS FRUIT. (a) Citrus fruit that
does not pass inspection prior to coloring shall be packed or
otherwise disposed of, in the presence of an inspector, without being
colored.
(b) The inspector may designate a time within usual packing
hours for the disposal of citrus fruit under Subsection (a) of this
section.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1250, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
