Sec. 13.463. PRACTICE BY LICENSE HOLDER. (a) A license holder
shall perform device maintenance activities in compliance with
department rules.
(b) A license holder may use only equipment approved by the
department, as provided by department rules, when performing device
maintenance activities.
Added by Acts 2013, 83rd Leg., R.S., Ch. 924 (H.B. 1494), Sec. 3.26,
eff. September 1, 2013.
