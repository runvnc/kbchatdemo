Sec. 61.008. NOXIOUS WEED CONTENT. The department by rule may
classify noxious weeds and establish the rate allowed or prohibit the
inclusion of a noxious weed in a container of agricultural or
vegetable seed.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1129, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
