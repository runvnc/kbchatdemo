Sec. 71.011. PROTECTION OF CARRIER FROM DAMAGES. A carrier,
including a railway, steamship, motorboat, bus, or truck, is not
liable to a consignor or consignee for damages for refusing to
receive and transport, or refusing to deliver across or into an area
protected by a quarantine, any fruit, plant, shrub, or other carrier
of an insect pest or plant disease in violation of an order or rule
of the department under this subchapter.
