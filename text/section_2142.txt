Sec. 301.202.
Statute text rendered on: 8/6/2022

ACT OF GOD.

If a person can establish that an

event that would otherwise be a violation of this chapter or a rule
adopted or order or permit issued under this chapter was caused
solely by an act of God, war, strike, riot, or other catastrophe, the
event is not a violation of this chapter or a rule, order, or permit
issued under this chapter.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
