Sec. 60.087. DIRECTOR'S BOND. (a) As soon as practicable
after a director is appointed or elected, the director shall execute
a $10,000 bond payable to the district and conditioned on the
faithful performance of the director's duties.
(b) Each director's bond must be approved by the board.
(c) The bond shall be filed with and retained by the district.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
