Sec. 41.023. NOTICE OF REFERENDUM AND ELECTION. (a) The
certified organization shall give public notice of:
(1) the date, hours, and polling places for voting in the
referendum and election;
(2) the estimated amount and basis of the assessment
proposed to be collected;
(3) whether a producer exemption is to be allowed in
accordance with Section 41.082; and
(4) a description of the manner in which the assessment is
to be collected and the proceeds administered and used.
