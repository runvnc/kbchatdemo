Sec. 143.025. SUBSEQUENT ELECTIONS TO ADOPT SUBCHAPTER. (a)
Except as provided by Subsection (b) of this section, if this
subchapter is not adopted at an election, another election for that
purpose may not be held in the county or area in which the election
was held earlier than one year after the date of the election.
