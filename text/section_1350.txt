Sec. 122.155. OPTIONAL POSTHARVEST TESTING. (a) The
department by rule shall allow a license holder to have a single
postharvest test performed on a representative sample of plants from
a plot if the results of the preharvest test representing the plot
show a delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol concentration of more than 0.3
percent on a dry weight basis.
(b) The department by rule shall prescribe the requirements for
a representative sample and for sample collection under this section.
(c) If a license holder fails to request postharvest testing on
or before the 15th day after the date the license holder is notified
of the results of the preharvest test, the results of the preharvest
test are final.
Added by Acts 2019, 86th Leg., R.S., Ch. 764 (H.B. 1325), Sec. 2, eff.
