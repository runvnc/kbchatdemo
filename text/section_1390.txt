Sec. 125.015. LIABILITY UNDER OTHER LAWS. (a) The provision
of information to an agricultural laborer does not in any way affect
the liability of an employer with regard to the health and safety of
a laborer or other person exposed to chemicals, nor does it affect
the employer's responsibility to take any action to prevent the
occurrence of occupational disease as required under any other
provision of law.
(b) The provision of information to an agricultural laborer
does not affect any other duty or responsibility of a manufacturer,
producer, or formulator to warn ultimate users of a chemical under
any other provision of law.
Added by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., ch. 903, Sec. 1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
