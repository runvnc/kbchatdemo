Sec. 95.015. NOTICE OF USE. A person shall notify the
department before using or permitting the use of coloring matter on
citrus fruit. If forms for that purpose are prescribed and furnished
by the department, the notice must be on those forms.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1249, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
