Sec. 18.071. AGRICULTURAL CERTIFICATION. The department may
establish certification programs under this subchapter relating to
the protection, sale, advertising, marketing, or related production
processes in this state.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 425, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
Amended by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 196, Sec. 3, eff. Sept. 1, 2003.
