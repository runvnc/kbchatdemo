Sec. 101.0151. BUYING OR SELLING BY WEIGHT. A licensee or a
person required to be licensed who buys or sells perishable
commodities by weight shall weigh or have the perishable commodities
weighed on scales that meet state requirements.
Added by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 358, Sec. 14, eff. Sept. 1, 1999.
Amended by:
Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 506 (S.B. 1016), Sec. 5.17, eff.
September 1, 2009.
