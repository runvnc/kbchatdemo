Sec. 301.059. GRANTS AND GIFTS. Subject to any limitations
imposed by law, the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation may
accept federal grants, private gifts, and donations from any other
source. Unless the use of the money is restricted or subject to any
limitations provided by law, the Texas Department of Licensing and
Regulation may spend the money for the administration of this
chapter.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
