Sec. 12.0204. ADVISORY COMMITTEES. (a) The department by rule
may establish advisory committees to make recommendations to the
department on programs, rules, and policies administered by the
department.
(b) In establishing an advisory committee under this section,
the department shall adopt rules, including rules regarding:
(1) the purpose, role, responsibility, goals, and duration
of the committee;
(2) the size of and quorum requirement for the committee;
(3) qualifications for committee membership;
