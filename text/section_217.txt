Sec. 14A.0115. GRAPE BYPRODUCTS. Notwithstanding any other
law, a natural byproduct of grapes stored in a public warehouse
operator's warehouse is an agricultural commodity and not subject to
regulation under other law, regardless of the byproduct's alcohol
content, until the byproduct is:
(1) removed from the warehouse; or
(2) mixed with another ingredient.
Added by Acts 2019, 86th Leg., R.S., Ch. 29 (S.B. 1939), Sec. 2, eff.
September 1, 2019.
