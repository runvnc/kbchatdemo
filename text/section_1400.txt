Sec. 128.011. DURATION OF NOTICE OF CLAIM OF LIEN. (a) Except
as otherwise provided by this chapter, the notice of claim of lien is
effective, and a new notice of claim of lien is not required to
maintain the lien, as long as the person who provides the
agricultural chemical, agricultural seed, or labor either:
(1) remains unpaid for the amount secured by the lien; or
(2) continues to provide an agricultural chemical,
agricultural seed, or labor on a regular basis to the lien debtor.
(b) For purposes of this section, providing an agricultural
chemical, agricultural seed, or labor is not considered to be made on
a regular basis if a period of more than 45 days elapses between
applications, deliveries, or preparations.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 197, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
