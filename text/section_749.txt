Sec. 63.073. PENALTY FOR LATE FILING OR PAYMENT. (a) If a
person does not file a report or pay the inspection fee as required
by Section 63.072, the registrant shall pay a penalty equal to 15
percent of the inspection fee due or $50, whichever is greater.
(b) A penalty, together with the delinquent inspection fee, is
due before the 61st day following the last day of the quarter, for
quarterly reports, or of August, for annual reports. The service
shall cancel the registration of a registrant who fails to pay the
penalty and delinquent inspection fee within that time period.
