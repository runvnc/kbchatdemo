Sec. 91.095. SNAP BEAN PACKS. Snap beans shall be packed in a
hamper weighing at least 17 pounds net weight per one-half bushel
when packed, or at least 34 pounds net weight per bushel when packed.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1226, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
