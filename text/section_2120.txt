Sec. 301.114. PROOF OF FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY. Proof of
financial responsibility is made by showing to the satisfaction of
the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation that the license
holder has the ability to respond in damages for liability that might
reasonably result from the operation for which the permit is sought.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
