Sec. 161.022. TERM. Commissioners serve for staggered terms of
six years, with the terms of four or five members expiring every
other year.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1395, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 699, Sec. 2, eff. Aug. 30, 1993;
Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1170, Sec. 2.02, eff. Sept. 1, 2003.
