Sec. 146.023. PAYMENT OF FEES AND DEBTS. The department shall
collect fees or debts owed to the state or to a supplier of goods or
services in connection with the processing of exported or imported
animals prior to the removal of the animals from the department's
facilities.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1368, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
