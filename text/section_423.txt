Sec. 50A.004. COMPOSITION. (a) The council is composed of the
following nine members:
(1) eight members who are appointed jointly by the
commissioner of public health and the vice chancellor for The Texas
A&M University System Agriculture Program; and

(2) one member who is a representative of the Department of
Agriculture and is appointed by the commissioner of agriculture.
(b) At least four members of the council who are appointed
under Subsection (a)(1) must each represent one of the following:
(1) the horticulture industry;
(2) the nutrition industry;
(3) the produce industry; or
(4) a local, county, or state health agency.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 180, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
