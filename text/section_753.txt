Sec. 63.092. PROCEDURE FOR SAMPLING AND ANALYSIS. The service
by rule shall prescribe the procedures for sampling and analysis of
commercial fertilizers. The procedures must, to the extent
practicable, be in accordance with the official methods of the
Association of Official Analytical Chemists or other methods that the
service considers authentic by research and investigation.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1146, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1851, ch. 349, art. 1, Sec. 6,
eff. Sept. 1, 1983.
