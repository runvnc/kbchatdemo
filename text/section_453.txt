Sec. 51.006. AREA OF OPERATION. A society shall confine its
activities and business operation to the community in which it is
located. Its activities and business operation may not extend beyond
the territory surrounding the town, village, or city designated as
the society's place of business.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1098, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
