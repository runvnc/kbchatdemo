Sec. 62.001. DEFINITIONS. In this chapter:
(1) "Board" means the State Seed and Plant Board.

AGRICULTURE CODE

(2) The term "certified seed" or "certified plant" means a
seed or plant that has been determined by a seed or plant certifying
agency to meet agency rules and standards as to genetic purity and
identity.
(3) "Plant" includes plant parts.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1134, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
