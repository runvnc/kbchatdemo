Sec. 92.034. PAYMENT OF CONTRIBUTIONS. (a) A packer or dealer
shall pay the contribution under this subchapter to the inspector who
inspects or grades the tomatoes.
(b) An inspector who renders an inspection or grading service
shall withhold delivery of the inspection certificate until the
contribution required under this subchapter is paid.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1233, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
