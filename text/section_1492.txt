Sec. 134.016. RECORDS. (a) The operator of a commercial
aquaculture facility shall maintain a record of sales of cultured
species for at least one year. The record is open for inspection by
designated employees of the Parks and Wildlife Department during
normal business hours.
(b) A record of sale for commercially protected finfish as
provided by Section 66.020, Parks and Wildlife Code, shall contain at
least the following information:
(1) invoice number;
(2) date of shipment;
