Sec. 141.123. WARNINGS. If the service determines that a
violation of this chapter is of a minor nature and that the public
interest will be served and protected by the issuance of a written
warning, the service may issue the warning instead of proceeding to
condemn the feed, reporting the violation for prosecution, or taking
other administrative action.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1332, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1876, ch. 349, art. 2, Sec. 8,
eff. Sept. 1, 1983.
