Sec. 144.122. ALTERING MARK OR BRAND. (a) A person commits an
offense if the person alters or changes a mark or brand on livestock
owned or controlled by that person without first having changed the
recorded mark or brand.
(b) An offense under this section is a misdemeanor punishable
by a fine of not more than $500.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1362, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
