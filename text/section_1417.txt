Sec. 128.048. APPLICABILITY OF OTHER LAW. Chapter 9, Business
& Commerce Code, applies to a lien created under this chapter to the
extent Chapter 9 is consistent with this chapter.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 197, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
