Sec. 72.022. CITRUS QUARANTINE ADVISORY COMMITTEE. (a) The
commissioners court of a county in a quarantined area shall appoint a

citrus quarantine advisory committee composed of four citrus growers
and one representative of the department. The four citrus growers
appointed by the court are subject to the approval of the department.
The department shall nominate its representative on the committee.
(b) If advised by the department that an infestation exists on
premises within the county, the advisory committee shall determine
the extent of the infestation and recommend to the department the
procedure for eliminating the infestation.
