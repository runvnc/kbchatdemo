Sec. 78.045. EQUIPMENT CLEANING PROCEDURE. (a) The board may
prescribe rules requiring the cleaning of and the disposal of
materials cleaned from farm implements and machinery brought into the
district or moved from one part of the district to another part.
(b) The board shall give notice of rules prescribed under this
section by:
(1) posting a copy of the notice of the adoption of the
rules at four public places in each county located in the district
not later than the 11th day before the effective date of the rules;
and
(2) filing a copy of the adoption of the rules with the
county clerk of each county located in the district.
(c) A person commits an offense if the person fails to obey a
rule prescribed under Subsection (a) of this section. An offense
under this subsection is a Class C misdemeanor.
