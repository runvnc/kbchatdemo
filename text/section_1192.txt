Sec. 93.025. RUSSET GRADE. Russet oranges, satsumas,
tangerines, and grapefruit have the same general qualities as bright
grade citrus fruit except russets have coloration that is rusty
brown.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1237, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
