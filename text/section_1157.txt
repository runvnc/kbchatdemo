Sec. 91.143. INSPECTION FEES. (a) A person requesting
inspection shall pay a fee for the inspection in an amount set by
rule of the department.
(b) The department shall set inspection fees at amounts that
are approximately equal to the cost of providing inspection and
classification services.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1228, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
