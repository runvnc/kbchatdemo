Sec. 91.043. CHOICE OR NO. 1 GRADE. (a) Choice or No. 1 Grade
peaches are:
(1) average in size and color for the variety;
(2) sound and firm, or properly mature for shipment to a
distant market; and
(3) practically free of blemishes.
(b) Choice or No. 1 Grade peaches shall be carefully picked and
closely packed in bushel baskets or in four-basket or six-basket
crates.
