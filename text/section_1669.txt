Sec. 149.005. PENALTY. (a) An offense under this chapter is
punishable by:
(1) a fine of not more than $1,000;
(2) confinement in jail for not less than 30 days nor more
than two years; or
(3) both the fine and confinement.
(b) A second or subsequent offense under this chapter is
punishable by imprisonment in the Texas Department of Criminal
Justice for not less than two years nor more than five years.
Added by Acts 1991, 72nd Leg., ch. 16, Sec. 2.01(a), eff. Aug. 26,
1991.
Amended by:
Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 87 (S.B. 1969), Sec. 25.005, eff.
September 1, 2009.
