Sec. 101.001. DEFINITIONS. In this chapter:
(1) "Handle" means buy for resale, sell, offer to sell,
process, broker, or ship for the purpose of selling.
(2) "Packer" means a person who prepares or packs
perishable commodities for barter, sale, exchange, or shipment.
(3) "Perishable commodity" means fresh produce grown in
Texas and generally considered a perishable vegetable or fruit.
(4) "Person" means an individual, partnership, group of
persons, corporation, or business unit.

(5) "Producer" means a person who is engaged in the
business of growing or producing any perishable commodity.
(6) "Warehouseman" means a person who receives and stores
perishable commodities for compensation.
