Sec. 46.006. REQUIREMENTS FOR PARTICIPATION. To be eligible
for participation in the program through the use of matching funds
under this chapter, an organization must:
(1) be an eligible applicant under Section 46.004 of this
chapter;
(2) prepare and submit a project request and application as
provided by department rule; and
(3) meet any other requirement established by department
rule.
Added by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 186, Sec. 2, eff. Sept. 1, 1999.
