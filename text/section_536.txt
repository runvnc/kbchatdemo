Sec. 55.006. RATIO OF CAPITAL TO LOANS. (a) The total amount
of the outstanding loans or discounts of an association formed under
this chapter may not exceed an amount equal to 10 times the amount of
the association's paid-up unimpaired capital stock.
(b) The articles of incorporation of an association formed
under this chapter must state the requirement of Subsection (a) of
this section.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1122, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
