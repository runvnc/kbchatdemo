Sec. 167.105. SEIZURE AND DISPOSAL OF ANIMALS MOVED IN
VIOLATION OF QUARANTINE. (a) An inspector who discovers animals
that are being or have been moved in violation of a quarantine may
request a peace officer to seize the animals and:
(1) impound the animals at the expense of the owner; or
(2) if practicable, return the animals at the expense of
the owner to the point of origin.
(b) In addition to other expenses, the owner of the seized
animals shall pay the officer a fee of $2 and the cost of feeding,
watering, and holding the animals.
