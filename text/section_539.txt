Sec. 55.009. FEES. (a) When the articles of incorporation of
an association formed under this chapter are filed, the incorporators
shall pay to the secretary of state a filing fee of $10.
(b) Each association formed under this chapter without capital
stock shall pay an annual license fee of $10.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1123, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
