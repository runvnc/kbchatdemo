Sec. 131.064. AFFIXING BRAND TO EQUIPMENT. A registrant shall
affix the registered brand to his or her apiary equipment by burning
or pressing the brand, in figures at least three-quarters of an inch
high, into the wood or other material in a manner that shows the
identification of equipment. The registrant shall affix the brand on
one or both ends of the hive. On other equipment, including a frame,
intercover, top, bottom, or plank, the registrant may affix the brand
in any place.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1884, ch. 350, Sec. 1, eff. Sept.
1, 1983.
