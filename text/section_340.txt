Sec. 41.207. ASSESSMENTS; APPLICABILITY OF OTHER LAW. (a) An
assessment levied on grain producers shall be applied by the board to
efforts relating to the indemnification of grain producers in this
state, including administrative costs of conducting an assessment
referendum.
(b) Assessments collected by the board are not state funds and
are not required to be deposited in the state treasury.
