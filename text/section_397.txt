Sec. 46.007. PROJECT REQUESTS. (a) A project request
submitted by an eligible participant must describe the advertising or
other market-oriented promotional activities to be carried out by the
department using matching funds.
(b) The department may not approve a project request submitted
under this section unless the request includes:
(1) a specific description of the project and how
assistance received under this chapter could be expended in
implementing the request;
