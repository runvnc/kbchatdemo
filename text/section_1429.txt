Sec. 131.022. QUARANTINES. (a) If the chief apiary inspector
determines that the public welfare requires the establishment of a
quarantine, the inspector may:
(1) declare a protective quarantine of a district, county,
precinct, or other defined area in which a disease of bees or a
deleterious exotic species of bees is not known to exist or in which
the disease or exotic species is being eradicated in accordance with
this subchapter; or
(2) declare a restrictive quarantine of a district, county,
precinct, or other defined area in which a disease of bees or a
deleterious exotic species of bees is located.
(b) A person may not move or ship bees, equipment, pollen, or
honey into or out of an area quarantined under this section, except
in accordance with rules adopted by the inspector.
