Sec. 59.014. APPROVAL BY ATTORNEY GENERAL. Before the bonds
delivered to the purchasers, the attorney general shall examine
record relating to the bonds. If the record demonstrates that
bonds have been issued in accordance with the Texas Constitution
this chapter, the attorney general shall approve the bonds.

Added by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 542, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1993.
