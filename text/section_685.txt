Sec. 60.131. REHEARING. On petition of a property owner
contesting an assessment of the owner's real property or agricultural
products received not later than the 30th day after the date the
assessment order is issued, the board may hold an additional hearing
to consider the assessment order.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
