Sec. 61.012. CANCELLATION OR REVOCATION OF AGRICULTURAL SEED
PERMIT. (a) The department may cancel an agricultural seed permit
issued under Section 61.011(c) of this code if the permittee fails to
observe the rules adopted, to file a report required, or to pay a fee
required under that section.
(b) The department shall revoke the permit of any person who
fails either to maintain records in this state or to pay for an outof-state audit under Section 61.011(e) of this code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1131, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
