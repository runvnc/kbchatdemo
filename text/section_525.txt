Sec. 54.008. RATIO OF CAPITAL TO LOANS. (a) A corporation
formed under this chapter shall automatically increase its capital
stock at the rate of five percent of the amount of loans made by the
corporation to shareholders of its common stock.
(b) The corporation's articles of incorporation and bylaws must
state the requirement of Subsection (a) of this section.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1120, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
