Sec. 14.081. OFFENSE IS VIOLATION; STANDARD OF PROOF. (a)
Commission of an offense under this chapter is also a violation for
purposes of administrative enforcement by the department.
(b) Proof of a violation under this chapter for purposes of
administrative enforcement, by assessment of an administrative
penalty or license sanction, is by a preponderance of the evidence.
(c) In an administrative enforcement action against a person
for the commission of an offense under this chapter, the department
is required to prove any intent element provided by the description
of the offense.
(d) Both an administrative enforcement action and a criminal
prosecution may be maintained against a person who violates this
chapter.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1124, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
