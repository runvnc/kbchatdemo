Sec. 165.002. TREATMENT. Except as otherwise provided by law,
a representative of the commission or a person authorized by the
commission may vaccinate, inoculate, or treat hogs with classical
swine fever virus or serum or with another remedy.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1428, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by:
Acts 2021, 87th Leg., R.S., Ch. 623 (S.B. 1997), Sec. 1, eff.
September 1, 2021.
