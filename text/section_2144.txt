Sec. 301.251. DEFINITION. In this subchapter, "permit holder"
includes each member of a partnership or association that is a permit
holder and, with respect to a corporation that is a permit holder,
each officer and the owner or owners of a majority of the corporate
stock, provided that the member or owner controls at least 20 percent
of the permit holder.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
