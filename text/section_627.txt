Sec. 60.002. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. This chapter furthers the
public purpose of improving the economy of this state by providing
incentives for the development of agricultural operations and
facilities.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
