Sec. 43.014. PUBLIC INSPECTION AND INFORMATION. (a) The
director shall at all reasonable times keep the experiment station
open to public inspection.
(b) The director shall disseminate information to the public
explaining the manner and methods of preparation, soil culture,
cultivation, gathering, preservation, and marketing the products of
the experiment station.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1095, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
