Sec. 52.035. FILING OF ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION. (a) The
incorporators shall file the articles of incorporation in accordance
with the general corporation laws of the state.

(c) If the association is formed with capital stock, the
incorporators are not required to obtain subscriptions or payment for
any part of the association's capital stock as a prerequisite of
filing the articles.
