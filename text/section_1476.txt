Sec. 132.046. SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR SHIPPED EGGS. Shipped
eggs coming into Texas in cartons ready for retail sale must be at
least Grade A, as established by a Texas licensee. Shipped eggs
coming into Texas loosed packed must be inspected and graded by a
Texas licensee at the licensee's place of business in Texas before
being sold at retail. All shipped eggs must be transported under
refrigeration in compliance with the rules of the department.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1319, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
