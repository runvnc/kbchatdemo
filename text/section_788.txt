Sec. 71.002. QUARANTINES AGAINST IN-STATE DISEASES AND PESTS.
If the department determines that a dangerous insect pest or plant
disease not widely distributed in this state exists within an area of
the state, the department shall quarantine the infested area.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1151, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
