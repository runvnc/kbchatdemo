Sec. 59.003. LIMITED IMMUNITY FROM SUIT OR LIABILITY. A member
of the board may be sued and held personally liable for damages that
result from an official act or omission only if the act or omission
is corrupt or malicious.
Added by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 542, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1993.
Amended by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 1014, Sec. 5, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
