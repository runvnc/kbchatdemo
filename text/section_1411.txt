Sec. 128.036. WRITTEN NOTICE TO SECURED CREDITORS BY LIEN
CLAIMANT. (a) A lien claimant shall provide written notice to any
secured creditor at least 30 days before the date the lien claimant
enforces a claim of lien.
(b) For purposes of this section, "secured creditor" means any
entity named as a secured party in a financing statement that is
filed regarding the debtor and that covers:
(1) a farm product, an account, or a crop subject to the
lien;
(2) a crop growing or grown on the land on which the
agricultural chemical was applied or for which the agricultural seed
was supplied; or
(3) the next crop to be grown on that land, if a crop is
not planted.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 197, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
