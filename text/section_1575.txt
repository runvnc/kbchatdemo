Sec. 143.054. EFFECT OF ELECTION. If a majority of the votes
cast are for the limited period of free range for hogs, after the
10th day following the date on which the proclamation is issued a
person may permit hogs to run at large in the county or area in which
the election was held during the period beginning on November 15 of
each year and ending on February 15 of the following year.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1346, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
