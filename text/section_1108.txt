Sec. 91.001. DEPARTMENT TO ADMINISTER. (a) The department
shall administer this chapter and adopt rules necessary for its
enforcement.
(b) The department may employ an assistant commissioner and
other personnel to supervise the operation of an inspection service
to carry out a cooperative agreement under Section 91.005 of this
code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1220, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 323, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1993.
