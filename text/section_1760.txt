Sec. 161.102. SUBMISSION OF SPECIMEN OF ANTHRAX VICTIM.
Immediately after pronouncing that an animal has died from anthrax,
as evidenced by a clinical or postmortem examination, a veterinarian
shall prepare and submit to the commission or a laboratory approved
by the commission:
(1) a suitable specimen from the animal;
(2) the name and address of the owner or caretaker of the
animal; and
(3) the location of the premises on which the animal died.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1402, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
