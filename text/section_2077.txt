Sec. 203.102. PROVIDE INFORMATION RELATING TO PROGRAM. The
board shall prepare and distribute information to each district
relating generally to the water supply enhancement program and
concerning the procedures for preparing, filing, and obtaining
approval of an application for cost sharing under Subchapter E.
Added by Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 655, Sec. 1, eff. Aug. 26, 1985.
Amended by:
Acts 2011, 82nd Leg., R.S., Ch. 61 (H.B. 1808), Sec. 18, eff.
September 1, 2011.
