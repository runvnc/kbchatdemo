Sec. 41.102. SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION OF LICENSE. In addition
to other remedies provided by law, a violation of any provision of
Subchapter B, C, D, E, H, or I is grounds for suspension or
revocation of any license or permit issued by the commissioner. The
suspension or revocation shall be conducted in accordance with the
procedures provided by law for suspension or revocation on the basis
of other grounds.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1090, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
