Sec. 50B.0025. ANNUAL PLAN AND BUDGET. Not later than November
1 of each year, the commissioner shall prepare for the current fiscal
year:
(1) a schedule and plan of action designed to implement and
further the objectives of this chapter and Chapter 110, Alcoholic
Beverage Code; and
(2) a budget and prioritized spending plan for expenditures
of the wine industry development fund.
Added by Acts 2015, 84th Leg., R.S., Ch. 846 (S.B. 880), Sec. 3, eff.
