Sec. 201.203. DESIGNATION OF LOCAL DISTRICTS. (a) The State
Soil and Water Conservation Board shall designate particular soil and
water conservation districts to administer certain programs.
(b) Before a local soil and water conservation district is
designated by the State Soil and Water Conservation Board, the
district must provide evidence to the State Soil and Water
Conservation Board that it is able to provide and to supervise
necessary technical assistance to landowners and operators within its
jurisdiction.
Added by Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 133, Sec. 3.02, eff. Nov. 5, 1985.
