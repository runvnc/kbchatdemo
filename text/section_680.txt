Sec. 60.126. ASSESSMENT ROLL. (a) The board shall prepare and
maintain an assessment roll showing the assessment against each
parcel of real property and against all applicable agricultural
products and the board's basis for the assessment.
(b) The board shall allow the public to inspect the assessment
roll.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
