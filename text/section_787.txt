Sec. 71.001. QUARANTINES AGAINST OUT-OF-STATE DISEASES AND
PESTS. If the department determines that a dangerous insect pest or
plant disease new to and not widely distributed in this state exists
in any area outside the state, the department shall establish a
quarantine against the infested area at the boundaries of the state
or in other areas within the state.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1151, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
