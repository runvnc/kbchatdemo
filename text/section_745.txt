Sec. 63.053.

LABELING OF CUSTOMER-FORMULA FERTILIZER.

Statute text rendered on: 8/6/2022

- 390 -

A person

AGRICULTURE CODE

distributing customer-formula fertilizer in this state shall at the
time of delivery furnish to the purchaser a label showing:
(1) the name and address of the purchaser;
(2) the date of sale;
(3) the grade of the mixture;
(4) the guaranteed analysis of the plant nutrients and
other additives;
(5) the net weight of the fertilizer; and
(6) the name and address of the registrant.
Added by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1846, ch. 349, art. 1, Sec. 3, eff.
Sept. 1, 1983.
