Sec. 93.032. PERMISSIBLE VARIATIONS. The standards established
under this subchapter may be varied to the extent that:
(1) there may be a 10 percent difference in size between
the citrus fruit on the top and the citrus fruit in the interior of a
pack; and
(2) there may be a 3 percent difference in quantity between
the actual count in a pack and the count prescribed.
