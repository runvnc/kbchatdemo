Sec. 44.008. COMPLIANCE. (a) On accepting a linked deposit,
an eligible lending institution must loan money to eligible borrowers
in accordance with the deposit agreement and this chapter. The
eligible lending institution shall forward a compliance report to the
board.
(b) The board shall monitor compliance with this chapter and
inform the comptroller of noncompliance on the part of an eligible
lending institution.
Added by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., 2nd C.S., ch. 32, art. 1, Sec. 1, eff.
