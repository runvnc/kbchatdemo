Sec. 74.005. ENTRY POWER; INSPECTIONS. For the purpose of
enforcing this chapter, the department is entitled to:
(1) enter any field of host plants or any premises in which
a host plant or its product is stored or held;
(2) examine any product, container, or substance
susceptible to cotton pest infestation; and
(3) examine the records of a purchaser, handler, or common
carrier of host plant products.
Added by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., ch. 691, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1987.
Amended by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 957, Sec. 5, eff. June 16, 1995.
