Sec. 13.038. SALE OF COMMODITY IN VIOLATION OF SUBCHAPTER. A
person violates this chapter if the person or the person's servant or
agent sells or keeps, offers, or exposes for sale a commodity in
violation of this subchapter.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1029, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by:
Acts 2013, 83rd Leg., R.S., Ch. 924 (H.B. 1494), Sec. 3.12, eff.
September 1, 2013.
