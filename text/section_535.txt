Sec. 55.005. LOANS. (a) Each applicant for a loan or discount
by an association formed under this chapter shall become a subscriber
to the association's capital stock in an amount equal to 10 percent
of the amount of the loan or discount for which application is made.
(b) The applicant shall pay for the stock required to be
purchased by Subsection (a) of this section at or before the time
that the loan is closed or the discount is granted.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1122, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
