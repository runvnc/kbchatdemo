Sec. 147.024. MORE THAN ONE PERSON UNDER SINGLE BOND. (a) If
two or more commission merchants are employees or agents solely for
one person, they may be covered by a single bond in an amount
determined under Section 147.023 of this code on the basis of
combined purchases and sales.
(b) Two or more commission merchants not subject to Subsection
(a) of this section may be covered under a single bond in an amount
not less than the aggregate amount of individual bonds required under
Section 147.023 of this code.
