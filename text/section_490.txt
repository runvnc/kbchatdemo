Sec. 52.036. EFFECT OF FILING ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION. When
the articles of incorporation are filed with the secretary of state,
all courts shall receive the articles or a certified copy of the
articles as prima facie evidence of:
(1) facts stated in the articles; and
(2) compliance with requirements for incorporation under
this chapter.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1107, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
