Sec. 122.360. CRIMINAL OFFENSE. (a) A person commits an
offense if the person violates Section 122.356.
(b) An offense under this section is a misdemeanor punishable
by a fine of not more than $1,000.
Added by Acts 2019, 86th Leg., R.S., Ch. 764 (H.B. 1325), Sec. 2, eff.
June 10, 2019.
