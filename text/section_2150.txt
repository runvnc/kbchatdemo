Sec. 301.257. OTHER RELIEF. A proceeding brought by the Texas
Department of Licensing and Regulation under this subchapter does not
affect the authority of the Texas Department of Licensing and
Regulation to bring suit for injunctive relief or a penalty, or both,
under this chapter.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
