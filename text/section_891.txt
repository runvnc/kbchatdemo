Sec. 73.007. PROTECTION OF CARRIER FROM DAMAGES. A
transportation company or common carrier is not liable for damages to
a consignor or consignee for refusing to receive for transportation
or refusing to deliver a citrus nursery product or citrus fruit, or a
package, bale, bundle, or box of that nursery product or fruit, that
is not accompanied by the certificate and permit required under
Section 73.006 of this code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1173, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
