Sec. 43.051. ESTABLISHMENT. For the purpose of aiding in the
development of the agricultural and horticultural resources of Texas,
any railway corporation operating in Texas may acquire, maintain, and
operate or cause to be operated demonstration and experiment farms,
orchards, and gardens.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1096, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
