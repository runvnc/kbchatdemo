Sec. 41.210. REIMBURSEMENT OF BOARD BY GRAIN BUYER; SUBROGATION
OF RIGHTS; REINSURANCE. (a) If the board pays a claim against a
grain buyer, the board is subrogated to the extent of the amount paid
to a grain producer by the board to all rights of the grain producer
against the grain buyer and any other entity from which the grain
producer is entitled to a payment for the loss giving rise to the
grain producer's claim under this subchapter.
(b) Funds recovered under this section shall be deposited with
the depository bank selected by the board under Section 41.060.
(c) The board may purchase reinsurance policies to mitigate the
board's financial risks.
Added by Acts 2011, 82nd Leg., R.S., Ch. 991 (H.B. 1840), Sec. 3, eff.
September 1, 2011.
Amended by:
Acts 2015, 84th Leg., R.S., Ch. 33 (S.B. 1099), Sec. 8, eff. May
19, 2015.
