Sec. 148.063. FAILURE TO KEEP OR PROVIDE RECORDS OF PURCHASE OR
SLAUGHTER. (a) A person required to keep records of purchase or
slaughter by Section 148.011 of this code commits an offense if the
person violates that section.
(b) An offense under this section is a misdemeanor punishable
by a fine of not more than $200.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1390, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
