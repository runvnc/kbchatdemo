Sec. 93.022. FANCY BRIGHT GRADE. Fancy bright oranges,
satsumas, tangerines, and grapefruit are:
(1) bright in color;
(2) shapely in form;
(3) practically free from skin defects and blemishes;
(4) fine in texture;
(5) reasonably thin;
(6) heavy and juicy; and
(7) free from frost damage.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1237, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
