Sec. 188.009. PERSON ENTITLED TO FILE; EXCEPTION. (a) Except
as provided by Subsection (b) or by Section 188.047, a person who
provides animal feed may file a notice of claim of lien as provided
by this chapter.
(b) The claimant may not file a notice of claim of lien if the
settlement of a dispute between the claimant and the debtor has been
submitted to the department and is pending.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 197, Sec. 2, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
Amended by Acts 1997, 75th Leg., ch. 292, Sec. 6, eff. Sept. 1, 1997.
