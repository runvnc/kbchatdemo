Sec. 131.061. IDENTIFICATION REQUIRED. A person may not
operate an apiary in this state unless the apiary equipment is:
(1) clearly and indelibly marked with the name and address
of the person; or
(2) branded in accordance with Section 131.064 of this code
with a brand registered to the person by the chief apiary inspector.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1884, ch. 350, Sec. 1, eff. Sept.
