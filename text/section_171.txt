Sec. 14.051. POSTING OF LICENSE. Each warehouse operator shall
immediately on receipt of a license post the original in a
conspicuous place at the primary recordkeeping location for the
individually licensed facility or combination. A copy of the license
must be conspicuously posted at each facility where grain is stored
for hire, handled for hire, or shipped for hire.
Renumbered from Sec. 14.008 and amended by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch.
1124, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
