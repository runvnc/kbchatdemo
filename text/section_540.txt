Sec. 55.010. EXEMPTION FROM FRANCHISE TAX. An association
formed under this chapter is exempt from all franchise or other
license taxes, except that:
(1) an association is not exempt from the annual license
fee under Section 55.009 of this code; and
(2) an association is exempt from the franchise tax imposed
by Chapter 171, Tax Code, only if exempted by that chapter.
