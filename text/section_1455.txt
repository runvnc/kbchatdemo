Sec. 131.123. LABELING OR SALE OF HONEY. (a) A person commits
an offense if the person violates a provision of Subchapter E of this
chapter.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class B misdemeanor.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1884, ch. 350, Sec. 1, eff. Sept.
1, 1983.
