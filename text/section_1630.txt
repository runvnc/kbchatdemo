Sec. 147.028. SUIT ON BOND. (a) Any person damaged by breach
of a condition of a bond may bring suit and recover under the bond.
The suit shall be brought in the county in which the bond is filed.
(b) A bond is not void on first recovery and may be sued on
until the total amount is exhausted. If a bond is reduced by onehalf, the commission merchant shall file a new bond, conditioned as
provided by this subchapter for the original bond, in an amount
necessary to restore the bond to the amount required by Section
147.023 of this code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1381, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
