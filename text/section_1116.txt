Sec. 91.008. PENALTY. (a) A person commits an offense if, as
a grower, shipper's agent, packer, or representative of a
transportation company, the person:
(1) refuses to allow an inspection under this chapter of
fruits or vegetables that are packed or ready for shipment; or
(2) violates a provision of this chapter.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class C misdemeanor.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1222, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1989, 71st Leg., ch. 230, Sec. 107, eff. Sept. 1,
1989.
