Sec. 71.206. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS. An institution of higher
education that conducts a plant pest and disease detection and
surveillance activity using money provided under this subchapter
shall, not later than the 90th day after the date the activity is
completed, submit to the department a report describing the purposes
and results of the activity.
Added by Acts 2021, 87th Leg., R.S., Ch. 27 (H.B. 2089), Sec. 1, eff.
September 1, 2021.
