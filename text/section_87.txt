Sec. 12A.152. EVALUATION OF EXAMINATION QUESTIONS. For each
licensing examination administered by the department, the department
shall periodically evaluate the effectiveness of examination
questions in objectively assessing an applicant's knowledge.
Added by Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 506 (S.B. 1016), Sec. 6.03,
eff. September 1, 2009.
