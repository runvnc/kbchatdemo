Sec. 63.125. SUIT TO RECOVER FEES. The service may sue to
recover an inspection fee or penalty due under Subchapter E of this
chapter. Venue for a suit under this section is in Brazos County.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1148, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Renumbered from Sec. 63.124 and amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p.
