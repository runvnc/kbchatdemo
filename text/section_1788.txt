Sec. 161.150. FAILURE TO REGISTER FERAL SWINE HOLDING
FACILITIES; HOLDING OF FERAL SWINE. (a) A person commits an offense
if the person recklessly:

(1) maintains a feral swine holding facility that is not
registered under Section 161.0412; or
(2) as the owner or person in charge of a holding facility
that is not registered under Section 161.0412, holds or permits
another to hold a feral swine in the holding facility.
(b) Each feral swine held or permitted to be held in violation
of Subsection (a)(2) constitutes a separate offense.
(c) An offense under this section is a Class C misdemeanor
unless it is shown on the trial of the offense that the defendant has
been previously convicted under this section, in which event the
offense is a Class B misdemeanor.
Added by Acts 2007, 80th Leg., R.S., Ch. 1242 (H.B. 2543), Sec. 16,
eff. September 1, 2007.
