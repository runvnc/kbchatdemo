Sec. 46.009. USE OF FUNDS. (a) Funds received under this
chapter may only be used for activities promoting the sale of Texas
agricultural products. The department by rule may allocate funds to
categories of eligible participants and to general or productspecific promotional activities. The department may use the funds in
an amount not to exceed $5,000 in a state fiscal year for the
purchase of food and beverage refreshments at "Go Texan" promotional
events.

(b) The department shall adopt rules to ensure that money in
the "Go Texan" partner program account is used only for the purposes
prescribed under this section.
(c) The payment of the administrative expenses under the
program may not exceed seven percent of the amount of the legislative
appropriation each biennium for the "Go Texan" partner program
account.
Added by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 186, Sec. 2, eff. Sept. 1, 1999.
Amended by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 208, Sec. 5, eff. May 21, 2001.
