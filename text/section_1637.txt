Sec. 147.064. APPROPRIATION OF PROCEEDS OF SALE. (a) A person
commits an offense if, as a livestock commission merchant, the person
appropriates or uses any portion of the net proceeds of the sale of
livestock for a purpose other than remitting the proceeds under
Section 147.004 of this code.
(b) A person commits an offense if, as a livestock auction
commission merchant, the person appropriates or uses any portion of
the net proceeds of the sale of livestock for a purpose other than
remitting the proceeds under Section 147.004 of this code.
(c) An offense under Subsection (a) is a felony punishable by
imprisonment in the Texas Department of Criminal Justice for not less
