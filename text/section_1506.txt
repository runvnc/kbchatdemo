Sec. 141.006. CUSTOM PROCESSING. This chapter does not apply
to the mixing, milling, or processing of a material produced by a
purchaser of commercial feed or acquired by the purchaser from a
source other than the person who mixes, mills, or processes the
material.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1325, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
