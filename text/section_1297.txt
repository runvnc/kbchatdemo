Sec. 102.171. PENALTY. (a) A person commits an offense if the
person:
(1) violates a provision of a marketing agreement or
license to which the person is subject; or
(2) engages in a transaction mentioned in and regulated by
a license to which the person is subject during the suspension or
after the revocation of the person's license.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class B misdemeanor.
(c) A person commits a separate offense for each day during
which the person acts under Subsection (a) of this section.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1274, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1989, 71st Leg., ch. 230, Sec. 120, eff. Sept. 1,
1989.
