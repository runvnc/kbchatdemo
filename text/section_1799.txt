Sec. 162.013. PENALTY FOR OWNER'S OR CARETAKER'S FAILURE TO
ASSIST. (a) A person commits an offense if, as the owner, part
owner, or caretaker of cattle, the person fails or refuses to
assemble the cattle or to provide assistance in accordance with
Section 162.010 of this code at the time and place provided in the
notice issued by the commission.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class C misdemeanor
unless it is shown on the trial of the offense that the defendant has
been previously convicted under this section, in which event the
offense is a Class B misdemeanor.
(c) A person commits a separate offense for each day of failure
or refusal.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1411, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1989, 71st Leg., ch. 836, Sec. 34, eff. Sept. 1,
