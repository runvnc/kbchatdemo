Sec. 79.002.

FILING OF BYLAWS.

Statute text rendered on: 8/6/2022

- 577 -

Under Article IV(h) of the

AGRICULTURE CODE

compact, copies of the bylaws adopted by the governing board and
amendments to the bylaws must be filed with the commissioner.
Added by Acts 2005, 79th Leg., Ch. 188 (H.B. 774), Sec. 1, eff.
September 1, 2005.
