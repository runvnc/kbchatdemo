Sec. 102.153. LIMITED APPLICATION OF SUBCHAPTER. This
subchapter applies only to areas of three citrus fruit producing
counties whose boundaries are contiguous and whose aggregate
population according to the last preceding federal census is not less
than 165,043. This subchapter does not apply to citrus fruit grown
in other areas of this state.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1268, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
