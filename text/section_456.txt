Sec. 51.009. ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION. (a) The incorporators
shall prepare articles of incorporation under the general corporation
laws of the state and shall deliver the articles to the attorney
general for approval.
(b) After the attorney general has approved the articles, the
incorporators shall file them with the secretary of state under the
general corporation laws of the state.
(c) The society shall file with the county clerk a certified
copy of the articles in accordance with Section 51.011 of this code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1098, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
