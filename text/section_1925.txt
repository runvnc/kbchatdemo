Sec. 168.007. NO FEE CHARGED. Neither the laboratory nor the
commission may charge a fee for testing or laboratory examination
provided for under this chapter.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1456, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1991, 72nd Leg., ch. 40, Sec. 2, eff. April 19, 1991;
Acts 1991, 72nd Leg., 1st C.S., ch. 14, Sec. 2.01, eff. Nov. 12,
1991.
