Sec. 131.046. DISPOSITION AND USE OF FEES. (a) Fees collected
under this subchapter shall be deposited in the State Treasury to the
credit of a special fund to be known as the bee disease control fund
to be used only to defray the costs of administering and enforcing
this chapter.
(b) The chief apiary inspector may sue to collect a delinquent
fee under this subchapter.
Amended by Acts 1983, 68th Leg., p. 1884, ch. 350, Sec. 1, eff. Sept.
1, 1983; Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 418, Sec. 3, eff. Sept. 1, 1985.
