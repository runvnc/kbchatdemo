Sec. 161.147. FAILURE TO MAINTAIN DEALER RECORDS. (a) A
person commits an offense if the person fails to maintain or permit
the inspection of a record required under Section 161.049 of this
code.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class B misdemeanor.
Added by Acts 1989, 71st Leg., ch. 836, Sec. 31, eff. Sept. 1, 1989.
