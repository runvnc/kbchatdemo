Sec. 131.010. FEES. The chief apiary inspector shall make a
reasonable effort to set the fees charged under this chapter at
amounts that will produce enough revenue to approximate 50 percent of
the inspector's total budget. In achieving this goal, the inspector
shall balance the revenue needs against the effect of the fees on the
industry.
Added by Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 418, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1985.
