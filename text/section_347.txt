Sec. 41.2145. REFUND OF ASSESSMENTS. (a) A grain producer who
has paid an assessment under Section 41.206 may be eligible for a
refund from excess money in the indemnity fund as provided by this
section.
(b) As a part of the annual budget proposal procedure described
by Section 41.059, the board shall review the budget for the next
year and the board's current financial status. Based on that review,
the board shall determine whether funds are available in excess of
the minimum fund balance to issue refunds to grain producers who paid
