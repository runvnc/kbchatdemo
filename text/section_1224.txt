Sec. 94.037. IMPORTED CITRUS FRUIT. The department may test
citrus fruit brought into this state from any outside area for
marketing or sale if there is reason to believe that the citrus fruit
does not comply with the maturity standards of this chapter for
similar citrus fruit produced in this state.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1245, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
