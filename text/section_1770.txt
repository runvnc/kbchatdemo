Statute text rendered on: 8/6/2022

VIOLATION BY CORPORATION.
- 910 -

If a corporation,

AGRICULTURE CODE

including a railroad company or a common carrier, violates a
provision of this chapter other than Section 161.048, Subchapter F,
or Subchapter G, the county attorney of the county in which the
offense occurs shall file and prosecute a civil suit against the
corporation on behalf of the state.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1404, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
