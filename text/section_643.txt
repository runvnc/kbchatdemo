Sec. 60.032. CONDUCT OF ELECTION. (a) The election shall be
held in accordance with the Election Code, to the extent not

inconsistent with this chapter.
(b) The ballot shall be printed to permit:
(1) voting for or against the proposition: "The creation
of __________ Agricultural Development District and confirmation of
the initial permanent directors of the district"; and
(2) naming of the proposed initial permanent directors.
(c) If the proposed district contains not more than 25
registered voters, Section 41.001(a), Election Code, does not apply
to the election.
