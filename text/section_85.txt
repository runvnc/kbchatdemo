Sec. 12A.101. REPLACEMENT LICENSE; FEE. The department shall
issue to a license holder whose license has been lost or destroyed or
whose name has been changed a replacement license if the license
holder submits to the department:
(1) an appropriate application; and
(2) a fee in an amount established by department rule.
