Sec. 93.005. REGISTERED BRANDS AND TRADEMARKS. (a) Brands and
trademarks and their United States grade definition shall, if
eligible, be registered with the department.
(b) To be eligible for registration, a brand or trademark must:
(1) be defined by the minimum requirements of a grade, or a
combination of grades, established under this chapter; or
(2) meet or exceed the requirements of U.S. No. 2 grade.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1236, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
