Sec. 74.0031. COTTON STALK DESTRUCTION. (a) Repealed by Acts
2021, 87th Leg., R.S., Ch. 848 (S.B. 703), Sec. 56(6), eff. September
1, 2021.
(b) The Texas Boll Weevil Eradication Foundation shall:
(1) conduct a study of the effects of incomplete cotton
stalk destruction and volunteer cotton control on boll weevil
eradication activities; and
(2) submit annual recommendations to the department and the
board of the foundation for a cotton stalk destruction deadline for
each pest management zone.
(c) The Texas Boll Weevil Eradication Foundation may consult
with its technical advisory committee in fulfilling its duties under
Subsection (b).
(d) The department shall set a cotton stalk destruction
deadline for each pest management zone, with consideration given to
the recommendations of the foundation.
Added by Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 178 (H.B. 1580), Sec. 2, eff.
May 27, 2009.
