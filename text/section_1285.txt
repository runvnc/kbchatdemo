Sec. 102.162. FEES. Each person applying for a marketing
agreement or license shall submit to the department a filing fee, as
provided by department rule, and a deposit in an amount that the
department considers sufficient and necessary to defray the expenses
of preparing and making effective the marketing agreement or license.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1271, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 419, Sec. 2.49, eff. Sept. 1,
1995.
