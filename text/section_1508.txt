Sec. 141.008. HEMP IN COMMERCIAL FEED. The service may adopt
rules authorizing, defining, and controlling the use of hemp and hemp
products in commercial feed.
Added by Acts 2019, 86th Leg., R.S., Ch. 764 (H.B. 1325), Sec. 3, eff.
June 10, 2019.
