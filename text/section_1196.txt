Sec. 93.029. PACKING STANDARDS. (a) Packed citrus fruit shall
be uniform in size.
(b) Oranges, satsumas, and tangerines shall be packed "stem-in,
twist" with the blossom end down in the first layer, and the stem end
down in all other layers.
