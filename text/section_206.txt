Sec. 14A.002. CERTIFICATE TO TRANSACT BUSINESS. No person may
operate a warehouse without first obtaining a certificate to transact
business as a public warehouse operator from the county clerk of the
county in which the warehouse is located.
Renumbered from Sec. 14.204 and amended by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch.
1124, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
