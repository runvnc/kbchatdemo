Sec. 74.1101. LIABILITY OF APPLICATORS. (a) In this section,
"applicator" means an individual or other person that is not a

member, director, officer, or employee of the foundation and that
contracts with the foundation to apply pesticides or other chemicals
using aircraft or other equipment to further or support the
eradication or diapause efforts undertaken under this subchapter.
(b) An applicator is not jointly and severally liable for any
act or omission of the foundation under this subchapter.
(c) The foundation shall have liability coverage in effect for
any eradication or diapause efforts for which it uses applicators.
The coverage shall apply to acts and omissions of the foundation and
volunteers and be in the amount of at least $500,000 for each single
occurrence of death, bodily injury, or property damage.
Added by Acts 1997, 75th Leg., ch. 463, Sec. 1.13, eff. May 30, 1997.
Amended by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 286, Sec. 2, eff. May 29, 1999.
