Sec. 143.122. REMOVAL OF FENCE BY OWNER. A person who owns an
interest in a fence attached to a fence owned in whole or in part by
another person is entitled to withdraw his or her fence from the
other fence after giving six months' notice of the intended
separation. The notice must be in writing and given to the owner of
the attached fence or to that person's agent, attorney, or lessee.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1351, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
