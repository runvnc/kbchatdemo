Sec. 164.023. METHOD OF TREATMENT. The commission by rule
shall prescribe the methods of treatment available for the treatment
of scabies under this chapter.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1422, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.

September 1, 2021.
