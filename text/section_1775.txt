Sec. 161.1375. MOVEMENT OF FERAL SWINE. (a) A person commits
an offense if the person recklessly:
(1) moves feral swine in a manner that is not in compliance

AGRICULTURE CODE

with rules adopted by the commission under Section 161.0412 or
161.054; or
(2) as the owner or person in charge of a holding facility
in which a feral swine is held, permits another to remove feral swine
from the holding facility in a manner that is not in compliance with
those rules.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class C misdemeanor for
each feral hog that is moved or permitted to be removed unless it is
shown on the trial of the offense that the defendant has been
previously convicted under this section, in which event the offense
is a Class B misdemeanor.
Added by Acts 2007, 80th Leg., R.S., Ch. 1242 (H.B. 2543), Sec. 16,
eff. September 1, 2007.
