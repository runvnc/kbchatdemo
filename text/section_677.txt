Sec. 60.123. HEARING AND PETITION REQUIRED. A district may
impose an assessment only if:
(1) a written petition has been filed with the board that:
(A) requests the assessment;
(B) states the specific purpose of the assessment; and
(C) is signed by each owner of the real property to be
assessed or, for an assessment on an agricultural product, by each
owner of real property on which the agricultural product subject to
the assessment may be produced;
(2) two-thirds of the members of the board vote to impose
the assessment;
