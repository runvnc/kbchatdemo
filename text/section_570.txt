Sec. 58.024. AUTHORITY EXEMPTION FROM TAXATION. The property
of the authority, its income, and operations are exempt from all
taxes and assessments imposed by the state and all public agencies
and political subdivisions on property acquired or used by the
authority under this chapter.
Added by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., 2nd C.S., ch. 32, art. 2, Sec. 1, eff.
Oct. 20, 1987.
