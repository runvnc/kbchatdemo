Sec. 74.121. REPORTS. Each person in an active eradication
zone growing cotton in this state shall furnish to the foundation on
forms supplied by the foundation information that the foundation
requires concerning the size and location of all commercial cotton
fields and of noncommercial patches of cotton grown for ornamental or
other purposes. The foundation may provide an incentive for early
and timely reporting.
Added by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 8, Sec. 1, eff. June 1, 1993.
Amended by Acts 1997, 75th Leg., ch. 463, Sec. 1.21, eff. May 30,
1997.
