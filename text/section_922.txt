Sec. 74.1091. SEPARATION OF RESPONSIBILITIES. The board shall
develop and implement policies that clearly separate the policymaking responsibilities of the board and the management
responsibilities of the chief executive officer and staff of the
foundation.
Added by Acts 2021, 87th Leg., R.S., Ch. 848 (S.B. 703), Sec. 23, eff.
September 1, 2021.
