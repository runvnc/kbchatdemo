Sec. 203.156. APPLICATION FOR COST SHARING. A person,
including a political subdivision of this state, that desires to
participate with the state in the water supply enhancement program
and to obtain cost-sharing participation by the state shall file an
application for a cost-sharing contract with the district board in
the district in which the land on which the contract is to be
performed is located. The application must be in the form provided
by board rules.
Added by Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 655, Sec. 1, eff. Aug. 26, 1985.
Amended by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 200, Sec. 13(m), eff. Sept. 1,
2003; Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 983, Sec. 10, eff. Sept. 1, 2003.
Amended by:
Acts 2011, 82nd Leg., R.S., Ch. 61 (H.B. 1808), Sec. 24, eff.
