Sec. 41.024. BASIS OF REFERENDUM AND ELECTION; ELIGIBLE
VOTERS. (a) Subject to the approval of the commissioner, the
certified organization may conduct the referendum and election under
this chapter either on an area or statewide basis, as determined by
the organization in its petition for certification.
(b) A producer of the agricultural commodity is eligible to
vote in the referendum and election if:
(1) the producer's production occurs within the area
described in the organization's petition; and
(2) the producer would be required under the referendum to
pay the assessment.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1083, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
