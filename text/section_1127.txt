Sec. 91.044. NO. 2 GRADE. Number 2 Grade peaches are all
peaches that are not good enough for No. 1 Grade but are sound,
suitable for market, and appropriate for reasonably distant shipment.
A No. 2 Grade peach may have slight defects, including:
(1) small size;
(2) a slightly uneven surface;
(3) green color; or
(4) ripeness.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1223, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
