Sec. 12A.001. APPLICABILITY OF PROVISIONS. The general
licensing, regulatory, and enforcement provisions of Chapter 12 and
this chapter apply to licensing and regulatory programs administered
by the department under any law.
Added by Acts 2009, 81st Leg., R.S., Ch. 506 (S.B. 1016), Sec. 6.03,
eff. September 1, 2009.
