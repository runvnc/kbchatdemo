Sec. 78.031. BOARD OF DIRECTORS. (a) The board of directors
of a noxious weed control district is composed of five persons, each
of whom must:
(1) hold title to land located in the district;
(2) be 18 years of age or older; and
(3) reside in a county all or part of which is located in
the district.
(b) Except as provided by Section 78.032 of this code, the term
of office of a director is two years.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1212, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
