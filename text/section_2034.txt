Sec. 201.131. PETITION FOR VARIANCE. (a) An owner or occupier
of land within a conservation district may petition the board of
adjustment of that conservation district to authorize a variance from
the terms of land-use regulations in the application of those
regulations to land owned or occupied by the petitioner.
(b) A petition for a variance must allege that there are great
practical difficulties or unnecessary hardships in the manner in
which the land-use regulations require the petitioner to carry out
the strict letter of those regulations.
