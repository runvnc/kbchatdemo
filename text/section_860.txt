Sec. 72.002. ADMINISTRATION; RULES. (a) The department shall
administer this chapter in order to control or eradicate the Mexican
fruit fly in this state and to protect all premises in this state
from that pest.
(b) The department may adopt rules as necessary for the
administration of this chapter.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1165, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by:
