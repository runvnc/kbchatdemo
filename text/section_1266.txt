Sec. 101.019. VENUE OF CIVIL OR CRIMINAL ACTION. The venue of
a civil action or criminal prosecution instituted under this chapter
is in the county in which the violation occurred, is occurring, or is
threatened or in which the perishable commodities were received by
the licensee, packer, or warehouseman.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1257, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1989, 71st Leg., ch. 230, Sec. 114, eff. Sept. 1,
1989; Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 358, Sec. 18, eff. Sept. 1, 1999.
