Sec. 78.032. INITIAL BOARD OF DIRECTORS. (a) The
commissioners court that ordered creation of the district shall
appoint five eligible persons to serve as the first board of
directors of the district.
(b) If the district is composed of land in more than one

(c) Three of the directors of the first board shall serve from

eligible voters, as authorized by Section 78.033 of this code, and
