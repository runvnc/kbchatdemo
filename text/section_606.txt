Sec. 7
(a) Except as provided by Subsections (b), (c), and (d) of this
section, proceeds from the sale of the bonds, other than refunding
bonds, shall be deposited in the state treasury to the credit of the
fund.
(b) The board may provide for transferring to the interest and

agricultural fund, to be administered as provided by Chapter 58 of
this code.
