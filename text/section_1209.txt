Sec. 94.001. DEFINITIONS. In this chapter:
(1) "Distributing house" means a place that receives or
ships, or a truck or railroad car that carries, citrus fruit that has
been shipped into this state from another.
(2) "Grove" means an area where citrus fruit is grown,
including a yard, garden, or orchard.
(3) "Packing house" means a place where citrus fruit is
packed or prepared to be marketed or transported.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1242, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
