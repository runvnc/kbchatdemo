Sec. 201.017. OATH; COMPENSATION. (a) Each member of the
state board shall take the constitutional oath of office.
(b) Each member of the state board is entitled to compensation
in an amount not to exceed $100 for each day of actual service
rendered. In addition, each member of the state board is entitled to
reimbursement for expenses, including traveling expenses, necessarily
incurred in the discharge of official duties.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1462, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
