Sec. 95.001. DEFINITION. In this chapter, "coloring matter"
means a dye, a liquid, a concentrate, a material containing a dye, or
a combination of materials that react to form a dye, that is used to
enhance the color of citrus fruit by the addition of artificial color
to the peel.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1247, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
