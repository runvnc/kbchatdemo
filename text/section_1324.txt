Sec. 111.003. IDENTIFICATION OF BALES. (a) Each ginner shall
mark each bale of cotton with the following:
(1) "B____________", filling the blank with the number of
the bale as shown on the books of the gin;
(2) the initials of each party who claims to own the
cotton; and
(3) an individual ginner's mark.
(b) The ginner's mark under Subsection (a) of this section
shall be placed under the initials of the parties claiming ownership.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1299, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
