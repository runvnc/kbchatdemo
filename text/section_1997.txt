Sec. 201.045. SUBSEQUENT PETITIONS. If the state board denies
a petition under this subchapter, a subsequent petition covering the
same or substantially the same territory may not be filed with the
board until six months have expired following the date of denial.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1467, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
