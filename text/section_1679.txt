Sec. 151.003. CRIMINAL PENALTY. (a) A person commits an
offense if the person knowingly violates Section 151.002(a).
(b) A first offense under this section is a Class C
misdemeanor. A subsequent offense under this section is a Class B
misdemeanor.
(c) The Texas Department of Health shall enforce Section
151.002 and shall file a criminal complaint against any person who
violates Section 151.002(a).
Added by Acts 1997, 75th Leg., ch. 954, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1997.
