Sec. 143.072. EXCEPTIONS; COUNTYWIDE ELECTIONS. The following
counties may not conduct a countywide election on the running at
large of cattle: Andrews, Coke, Culberson, Hardin, Hemphill,
Hudspeth, Jasper, Jefferson, Kenedy, Kinney, LaSalle, Loving, Motley,
Newton, Presidio, Roberts, Schleicher, Terry, Tyler, Upton, Wharton,
or Yoakum.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1348, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
