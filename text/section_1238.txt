Sec. 95.018. VARIATION FROM LICENSED COLORING MATTER. A
licensee or other person may not manufacture or use coloring matter
that contains an ingredient that is prohibited under this chapter or
that varies materially from the formula on file with the department.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1249, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
