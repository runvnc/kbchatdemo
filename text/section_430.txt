Sec. 50A.011. FINANCES. (a) The council may accept, for the
purposes of this chapter, gifts and grants from public and private
sources, subject only to limitations contained in the gift or grant.
(b) Funds appropriated for the purposes of this chapter shall
be expended at the direction of the council on grants approved by a
majority of the council.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 180, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
