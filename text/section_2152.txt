Sec. 301.302. PRIVATE LEGAL RELATIONSHIPS. (a) This chapter
does not affect private legal relationships, except that an operation
conducted under the license and permit requirements of this chapter
is not an ultrahazardous activity that makes the participants subject
to liability without fault.
(b) The fact that a person holds a license or permit under this
chapter or that the person has complied with this chapter or the
rules issued under this chapter is not admissible as evidence in any
legal proceeding brought against the person.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
