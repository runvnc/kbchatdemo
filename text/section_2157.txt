Sec. 302.005. CONTRACTS. The department may enter into
contracts with public or private entities to assist the department in
the administration or evaluation of the weather modification and
control grant program or to conduct research relating to the
effectiveness of weather modification and control activities.
Added by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 1276, Sec. 2.001(a), eff. Sept. 1,
2003.
