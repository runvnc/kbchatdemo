Sec. 91.026. BERRY BOX OR CRATE. (a) A quart berry box or
crate must hold at least 24 quart baskets, each of which must contain
at least 67.2 cubic inches dry measure.
(b) A pint berry box or crate must hold at least 24 pint
baskets, each of which must contain at least 33.6 cubic inches dry
measure.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1222, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
