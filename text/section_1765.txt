Sec. 161.114. INSPECTION OF LIVESTOCK. An authorized inspector
may examine livestock consigned to and delivered on the premises of a
livestock market before the livestock are offered for sale. If the
inspector considers it necessary, the inspector may have an animal
tested or vaccinated. Any testing or vaccination must occur before
the animal is removed from the livestock market.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1403, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by:
