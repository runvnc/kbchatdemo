Sec. 60.062. POWERS BEYOND DISTRICT TERRITORY. The district
may exercise any of its powers outside the boundaries of the
district, except the power to impose assessments and the power of
eminent domain, if the board determines that there is a benefit to
the district in exercising that power.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
