Sec. 51.018. SUSPENSION; EXPULSION. (a) As prescribed by the
society's bylaws, a society may suspend or expel a member for
misconduct.
(b) If a member is expelled, the society shall return to the
member, at a time provided by its bylaws, money in an amount equal to
the value of the member's contribution to the society's working
capital.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1100, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
