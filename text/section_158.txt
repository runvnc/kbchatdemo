Sec. 14.021. LICENSE REQUIRED. A person may not operate a
public grain warehouse without first obtaining from the department a
license in the person's name covering the warehouse.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1047, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Renumbered from Sec. 14.004 and amended by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch.
1124, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
