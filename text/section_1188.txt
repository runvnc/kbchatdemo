Sec. 93.021. CONTAINER STANDARDS. (a) Citrus fruit shall be
packed in closed containers that are approved by the department.
(b) The standard orange box is 12 by 12 by 12 inches, and the
standard one-half orange box is 12 by 12 by 6 inches, both sizes
being measured on the inside.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1237, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
