Sec. 143.108. PENALTY. (a) A person commits an offense if the
person violates Section 143.102 of this code.
(b) An offense under this section is a Class C misdemeanor.
(c) A person commits a separate offense for each day that an
animal is permitted to roam at large in violation of Section 143.102
of this code.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1350, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., ch. 51, Sec. 4, eff. Sept. 1, 1987.
