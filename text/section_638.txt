Sec. 60.027. TEMPORARY DIRECTORS; VACANCY IN OFFICE. (a) If
the commissioners court grants the petition, it shall appoint as
temporary directors of the district five persons who are qualified
under this chapter to serve as directors.
(b) A vacancy in the office of temporary directors shall be
filled by appointment by the commissioners court.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 1393, Sec. 1, eff. June 16, 2001.
