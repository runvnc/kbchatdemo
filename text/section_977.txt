Sec. 76.072. EXPIRATION. A pesticide dealer license expires on
the second anniversary of the date of its granting or renewal unless
the department by rule adopts a system under which licenses expire on
specified dates during a year.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1195, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
Amended by Acts 1997, 75th Leg., ch. 1369, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1,
1997.
