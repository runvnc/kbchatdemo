Sec. 188.018. RECOGNITION OF NOTICE AS FINANCING STATEMENT.
The secretary of state shall recognize a notice of claim of lien
under this subchapter as a financing statement under Subchapter E,
Chapter 9, Business & Commerce Code.
Added by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 197, Sec. 2, eff. Sept. 1, 1995.
Amended by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 414, Sec. 2.08, eff. July 1,
2001.
