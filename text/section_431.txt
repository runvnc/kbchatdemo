Sec. 50A.012. REPORTING. (a) The Texas Agricultural
Experiment Station shall prepare annually a complete and detailed
written report about:
(1) all funds received and disbursed by the council during
the preceding fiscal year;
(2) the council's progress through funded projects in
improving the health benefits of produce grown in this state;
(3) the development of an information system or network to
share health benefit information with state agencies responsible for
feeding programs and for distributing dietary information to
consumers in this state; and
(4) recommendations for improving the health of consumers
through increased consumption of this state's produce.
(b) The part of the annual report required by Subsection (a)(1)
must meet the reporting requirements applicable to financial
reporting provided in the General Appropriations Act.
(c) The annual report shall be submitted to the governor, the
lieutenant governor, the speaker of the house of representatives, the
commissioner of agriculture, the commissioner of public health, and
the vice chancellor for The Texas A&M University System Agriculture
Program.
Added by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch. 180, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
