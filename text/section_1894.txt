Sec. 167.103. TREATMENT OF ANIMALS BY PEACE OFFICER ON REQUEST
OF INSPECTOR. (a) If a person responsible for treating animals
fails to treat the animals at the time and place directed in the
order or, prior to a treatment date in the order, states that he or

(b) The peace officer shall deputize a sufficient number of

and shall gather and treat the animals under the supervision of an

(c) The peace officer shall continue to treat the animals on
each treatment date specified in the order until the person
responsible for treatment begins and continues the treatment in
accordance with that order.
