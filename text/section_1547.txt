Sec. 142.004. REDEMPTION. (a) The owner of the estray may
redeem the estray from the owner or occupant of public or private
property if:
(1) the owner of the estray and the owner or occupant of
the property agree to a redemption payment amount and the owner or
occupant of the property receives the redemption payment from the
owner of the estray; or
(2) a justice court having jurisdiction determines the
redemption payment amount and gives the owner of the estray written
authority to redeem the estray under Section 142.006.
(b) If the owner of the estray does not redeem the estray not
later than the fifth day after the date of notification, the sheriff
or the sheriff's designee shall proceed immediately with the
impoundment process prescribed by Section 142.009 unless the sheriff
or the sheriff's designee determines that the owner of the estray is
making a good faith effort to comply with Subsection (a). During the
impoundment process period, the estray may not be used for any
purpose by the owner or occupant of the property.
