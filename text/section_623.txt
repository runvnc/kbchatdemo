Sec. 59.032. UNENCUMBERED TITLE. The board may establish a
procedure by which a borrower acquiring land with a loan under this
chapter may obtain title to a portion of the tract clear of
encumbrances.
Added by Acts 1993, 73rd Leg., ch. 542, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1993.
Amended by Acts 1995, 74th Leg., ch. 1014, Sec. 20, eff. Jan. 1,
1996.
