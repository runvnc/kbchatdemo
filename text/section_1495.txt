Sec. 134.020. EXOTIC SPECIES. (a) The Parks and Wildlife
Commission shall adopt rules regulating the importation, possession,
propagation, and sale of harmful or potentially harmful exotic
species by an aquaculturist.
(b) The Parks and Wildlife Commission, after consulting with
the commissioner and an individual designated by the chairman of the
board of regents of The Texas A&M University System, shall determine
and publish a list of harmful or potentially harmful exotic species
that an aquaculturist may not import, possess, or sell as part of the
person's aquaculture activities.
(c) An aquaculturist may not release in public water harmful or
potentially harmful exotic species except as provided by Section
66.007, Parks and Wildlife Code.
(d) The Parks and Wildlife Department shall enforce the rules
adopted under this section.
