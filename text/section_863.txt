Sec. 72.006. PROSECUTIONS. On request of the department, an
enforcement officer, or another interested person, the district or
county attorney of any county in which a violation of a provision of
this chapter occurs shall prosecute the violation.
Acts 1981, 67th Leg., p. 1165, ch. 388, Sec. 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1981.
