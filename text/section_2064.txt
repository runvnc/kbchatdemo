Sec. 203.012. RULES. The board, after consulting with local
districts, shall adopt reasonable rules that are necessary to carry
out this chapter.
Added by Acts 1985, 69th Leg., ch. 655, Sec. 1, eff. Aug. 26, 1985.
Amended by Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 200, Sec. 13(e), eff. Sept. 1,
2003; Acts 2003, 78th Leg., ch. 983, Sec. 6, eff. Sept. 1, 2003.
