Sec. 92.001. POLICY. It is in the interest of the public
welfare of this state to provide growers, shippers, carriers,
receivers, and consumers with evidence of the quality, quantity, and
condition of tomatoes they grow, ship, or purchase. The purpose of
this chapter is to authorize and prescribe the procedures by which
growers and shippers of tomatoes may secure prompt and efficient
