Sec. 50.002. TERM OF AWARD. An award under Section 50.001 must
be granted for a two-year period and may be extended at the end of
that period after review.
Added by Acts 1999, 76th Leg., ch. 1074, Sec. 2, eff. June 18, 1999.
Renumbered and amended from Sec. 46.002 by Acts 2001, 77th Leg., ch.
1420, Sec. 21.001(3), Sec. 21.002(1), eff. Sept. 1, 2001.
