Sec. 52.006. DURATION OF EXISTENCE. Each association
incorporated and organized under this chapter after August 1, 1987,
has perpetual existence unless a limited duration is provided for and
stated in its charter or articles of incorporation or in an amendment
to either of the documents. Each association incorporated and
organized under this chapter that is in existence on August 1, 1987,
has perpetual existence unless a limited duration is provided for and
stated in an amendment to its charter or articles of incorporation.
Added by Acts 1987, 70th Leg., ch. 695, Sec. 1, eff. Aug. 31, 1987.
